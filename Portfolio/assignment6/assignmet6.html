Jeff The Land Shark Buff Opinions

Aromatic-Ordinary-97

I’m not gonna lie—Jeff’s ultimate needs a buff. Right now, it’s way too easy to dodge. Characters like Dagger can use their abilities to avoid it, and not just them, but their teammates too. I’ve lost so many fights because of this. Back when Marvel Rivals first came out, Jeff was my main. His ultimate used to be much more effective—you could hit 4 or 5 enemies easily. But now, I’m lucky if I get 3. It just doesn’t feel as strong anymore. And here’s something wild—Jeff can actually dodge another Jeff’s ult! If you time it perfectly and burrow underground just before the enemy Jeff starts his ult and his ult start eating everyone, you can avoid it completely. I’ve tested it, and it’s crazy.

How to Buff Jeff: Make his ult harder to dodge. Some characters should still be able to avoid it, but right now, over 35% of the charters can counter it—that’s too many. Improve his healing. Jeff’s healing is one of the weakest in the game. His ultimate should create a rain cloud that heals teammates. Fix the bubbles. Either increase the number of bubbles or lower the cooldown when picking one up. Bonus Idea (Not a Buff, but Would Be Awesome!): There should be a feature where you can pet Jeff. Imagine an emote where, if Jeff activates it, other players can walk up, press a button, and pet him. That would be adorable! That’s my idea—hope you like it!
